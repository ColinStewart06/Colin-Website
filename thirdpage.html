1. A chef's knife is a versatile tool, perfect for chopping, dicing, and slicing large ingredients with precision. It has a broad, sharp blade for effortless cutting and balanced handling.

2. A paring knife is small with a narrow blade, designed for delicate tasks like peeling, trimming, and precision cutting. Its maneuverability makes it ideal for intricate food preparation.

3. A serrated knife features a jagged edge, perfect for cutting through crusty bread, tomatoes, or other foods with tough exteriors and soft interiors, ensuring clean, easy cuts without squashing.
